The SDU Winter School for Teachers has ended

The SDU Winter School for school teachers has recently ended. The 72-hour course was dedicated to the topic “Renewed content of education”, it was held online from February to March 2022.

Academic mobility announcement

The acceptance of documents under the academic mobility program at the expense of funding from the Ministry of Education and Science of the Republic of Kazakhstan for the autumn semester of the 2022-2023 academic year is open.

SDU scientist received a certificate of honor from Askhat Aimagambetov

On the eve of the Day of Science, Associate Professor of SDU Madina Alimanova received a Certificate of Honor from the Minister of Education and Science of the Republic of Kazakhstan Askhat Aimagambetov. Madina Ungarovna - PhD, science coordinator at the Faculty of Engineering and Natural Sciences, founder of the 3D laboratory.

SDU in World Ranking "THE"

For the first time, SDU was included in the world ranking Times Higher Education Impact Ranking. We are pleased to announce that SDU has been included in the Global Times Higher Education Impact Ranking along with 10 Kazakh universities and 1396 global universities.

SDU students won the Olympiad Cup

Suleyman Demirel University students took first place in the XIV Republican Subject Olympiad in the direction of "Journalism" and became the owners of the challenge cup. Next year the competition will be held in SDU. Today Rector of the University Davronjon Gaipov congratulated the winners of the competition.

Meeting of the Board of Trustees of the educational program "Journalism"

A meeting of the Board of Trustees of the educational program "Journalism" of the University named after Suleyman Demirel was held. The meeting, organized by the Faculty of Law and Social Sciences, was attended by representatives of the country's leading media. The participants of the meeting got acquainted with the content of the current educational program and spoke about the necessary changes in the educational process. They also discussed such important issues as training for the modern market.

Meeting of the Advisory Board

A meeting of the Advisory Council on the educational program "Information Systems" was held in SDU. The work of the council is aimed at discussing with teachers the content of educational programs at the university, the skills needed by graduates that are in demand in the market, internships and employment.
